About the Game

Welcome to the TV Network Executive Simulator! In this game, you'll experience 20 years as a TV network executive making crucial decisions about your programming.

How to Play

  • Each year, you must choose between ordering a new pilot or renewing your current show
  • New shows (pilots) are a gamble - they could be hits or flops
  • Existing shows tend to gradually decline in ratings, but are more predictable

Scoring

  • Ratings: Shows are rated from 0 to 10
  • Total Rating: The sum of all your ratings across 20 years
  • Average Rating: Your total rating divided by 20

Strategy Tips

  • Balance the risk of new pilots against the safety of renewals
  • Consider when to end a declining show versus risking a new pilot
